Watch this short scenario to understand how buying and selling scams happen.

Watch the video

Key message: If a deal looks too good to be true, stop and check before you pay.

What you learned

  • Scammers can pretend to be real buyers, sellers, or trusted online shops.
  • They often use very low prices or urgent messages to pressure you.
  • You should never send money before checking the person, item, or website properly.
  • Always use trusted payment methods and inspect expensive items when possible.
