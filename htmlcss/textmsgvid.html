Watch the video

Key message: Do not click links in unexpected text messages. Stop, check, and delete.

What you learned

  • Scammers can send messages that look like they are from real companies or people you know.
  • They often try to rush you into clicking a link or sharing personal details.
  • You should never trust a message just because it looks official.
  • Always check using a trusted phone number or official website.
