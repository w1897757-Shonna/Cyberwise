Key message: Always check payment details with a trusted phone number before sending money.

What you learned

  • Scammers can pretend to be a real company you trust.
  • They may send emails asking you to pay into a different bank account.
  • Email addresses can look real but have small changes.
  • Always stop, check, and call before making a payment.
